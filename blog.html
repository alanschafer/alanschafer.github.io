Trauma on the Brain: The Neurobiological Effects of PTSD | Daisy Payton | TEDxMeritAcademy

This talk delves into how trauma changes the brain’s structure and chemistry, affecting memory, emotional regulation, and daily functioning. It explores the scientific basis for understanding PTSD and why awareness is crucial for healing.

A.C. Grayling – How Belief Systems Work

A.C. Grayling examines the mechanisms behind belief systems — how they emerge, sustain themselves, and shape societies. This discussion challenges the viewer to think critically about the origins and influence of deeply held ideas.

The Bible Has No Inherent Meaning

An exploration of the historical, cultural, and philosophical context behind claims of the Bible’s value. This presentation examines the origins of the text, the impact of interpretation over time, and why its significance depends on the perspective of the reader.
