About VON

Founded in 2018, VON was established to provide clear, unbiased, and deeply researched analysis in an era of unprecedented complexity. Our multidisciplinary team of experts spans the fields of political science, economics, computer science, and national security.

We are committed to intellectual independence and believe that a better understanding of the forces shaping our future is essential for creating a more stable and prosperous world. Our work is non-partisan and is intended to inform policy, guide investment, and educate the public.
